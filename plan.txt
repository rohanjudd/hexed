Hexed Game

Concept:
Game that challenges to you to enter the binary representation of hex values and vice versa. 

Components:
Arduino Nano Clone
Two 32*128 Oleds with SSD1306 I2C Drivers
8 tactile switches
rotary encoder
piezo buzzer
18650 battery with boost charge circuit salvaged from power bank
power switch
power led

Build Plan
Get basic game functions working over serial with a nano
Draw Schematic
Wait for parts
solder onto proto board using headers for screens and nano
Get concept working
Make and order pcb
3d print case
Assembly and final software
